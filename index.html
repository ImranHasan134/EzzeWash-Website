Terms & Privacy Policy

1. Service Agreement

By placing an order with EzzeWash, you agree to our service terms. We reserve the right to refuse service for items that are heavily soiled, damaged beyond normal care, or present a risk to our equipment or staff.

2. Garment Care & Liability

EzzeWash takes utmost care with every garment. However, we are not responsible for pre-existing damage, fading, or shrinkage due to the fabric's inherent nature. Customers must declare valuable or delicate items before submission.

3. Turnaround Time

Standard service: 2–3 business days. Express service: 24 hours (subject to availability). EzzeWash will notify customers of delays. We are not liable for delays caused by force majeure.

4. Lost or Damaged Items

In the rare event of loss or significant damage caused by our negligence, EzzeWash will offer compensation up to 10× the service charge paid, not exceeding the item's declared value.

5. Pricing & Promo Codes

Prices are subject to change without prior notice. Promo codes cannot be combined and are valid for stated periods only. Expired codes will not be honored.

6. Pickup & Delivery

Delivery is available within specific zones in Dhaka. Delivery charges may apply. EzzeWash reserves the right to reschedule pickups or deliveries with prior notification.

1. Data Collection

EzzeWash collects name, phone number, address, and payment information necessary to fulfill your orders. We do not collect sensitive data beyond what is required for our services.

2. Data Storage & Security

All personal data is stored on encrypted servers. We use industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit. Your data is never sold to third parties.

3. Data Sharing

We may share your delivery address with our logistics partners solely for order fulfillment. Payment data is handled by PCI-compliant payment processors and is never stored on our servers.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data. Contact us at privacy@ezzewash.com to exercise these rights.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve user experience and analyze traffic. You may disable cookies in your browser, though some features may be affected.

1. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are available if: (a) we fail to complete the service within the agreed timeframe, (b) items are lost or significantly damaged due to our negligence, or (c) incorrect service was applied.

2. Cancellation Policy

Orders may be cancelled free of charge up to 2 hours before the scheduled pickup. Cancellations after pickup has occurred will incur a 20% processing fee.

3. Refund Process

Approved refunds will be processed within 5–7 business days. Card refunds will appear on your statement within 7–10 banking days. bKash/Nagad refunds are typically instant upon approval.

4. How to Request

Contact us at refunds@ezzewash.com or call our helpline within 48 hours of delivery with your order ID and description of the issue. Include photos of damaged items if applicable.
